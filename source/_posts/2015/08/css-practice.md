## 1. 善用 伪元素(Pseudo-elements)

* `:before`
* `:after`
* `:first-child`
* `:last-child`

### 实例

* 插入类似列表符号：[JsFiddle](https://jsfiddle.net/pg4kpc3k/)

* 插入 clearfix：[JsFiddle](https://jsfiddle.net/hy4av6eu/)
